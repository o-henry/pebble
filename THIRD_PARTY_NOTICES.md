## DM Mono Nerd Font

ScreenPebble bundles the Light, Regular, and Medium faces of DM Mono Nerd Font
for its local interface.

- Base font: [DM Mono](https://github.com/googlefonts/dm-mono)
- Glyph patch: [Nerd Fonts](https://github.com/ryanoasis/nerd-fonts)
- License: SIL Open Font License 1.1

The source copy is included at [src/assets/fonts/OFL.txt](src/assets/fonts/OFL.txt).
The packaged frontend also includes the same notice at
`public/licenses/DM-Mono-Nerd-OFL.txt`.
